Based on available FreeSurfer normative databases and publications, I've compiled relative volume data (as fractions of total intracranial volume/eTIV) for the requested ROIs 
. The primary sources include FreeSurfer subcortical normative databases and large-scale neuroimaging studies using FreeSurfer segmentation 
.

Key limitations:

Most studies report volumes in mm³ rather than relative fractions, requiring conversion using typical eTIV values (approximately 1,450,000 mm³ for females and 1,580,000 mm³ for males) 
Data for some ROIs like Basal Forebrain and Ventral DC are scarce in published normative datasets 
Age-related atrophy patterns show significant variability, with hippocampus declining ~0.5-1% per year after age 60 
Sexual dimorphism exists even after eTIV correction, particularly for amygdala and hippocampus
