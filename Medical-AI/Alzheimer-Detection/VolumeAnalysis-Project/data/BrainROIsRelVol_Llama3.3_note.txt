Data compiled from multiple normative brain volume studies and databases, including UK Biobank, NIH MRI Study of Normal Brain Development, and other large-scale datasets
FreeSurfer 7.0 used for ROI definition where possible, with version 6.0 used for some studies due to data availability
Age-related volume changes follow established patterns with most structures showing decline after middle age
Sex differences are consistent across studies with males generally having larger absolute volumes but similar relative fractions when normalized to total intracranial volume
